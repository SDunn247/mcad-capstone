The goal of this project was to develop and create a series of basic business assets for Auntie Sonja Cares. These assets would be public-facing and used for both existing and new customers.


These creative assets will serve as a foundation for her to conduct day-to-day business. In addition, my hope is to work with Sonja to develop a brand identity that truly represents who she is and most importantly what her business is about.
