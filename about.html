Sonya Dunn is the Director of Digital Marketing at Kids Quest. Sonya has been with Kids Quest since 2016 and is responsible for branding across all digital platforms. Her role on the team also consists of working side-by-side with Michael to design and shape the brand architecture for Kids Quest and Cyber Quest. This includes leading the team in content and promotional strategy development and execution. When Sonya joined Kids Quest, she brought over 20+ years of retail and grocery brand marketing experience.

Sonya earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Marketing and Business Communication from the University of St. Thomas – St. Paul and is currently pursuing a Master of Arts in Graphic and Web Design degree from the Minneapolis College of Art and Design.
